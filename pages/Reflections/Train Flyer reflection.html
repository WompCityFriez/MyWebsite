Reflection on a Flyer for train ride


About This Project

This assignment taught me the significance of typography and how font choices can evoke a specific time period, like the 1920s. It enhanced my understanding of how typography can influence design and how various text modifications can accentuate a topic. Additionally, I gained valuable experience with Adobe Illustrator, particularly in manipulating and transforming text. Although challenging at times, this assignment improved my creativity and resilience when tackling difficult tasks.

While creating the train flyer for my Interactive Media CTE class, I learned how to effectively combine design principles with functionality. I focused on creating a visually appealing layout that communicated the necessary information clearly while also maintaining a cohesive theme. I gained hands-on experience with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, learning how to manipulate images, adjust typography, and use colors strategically to enhance the flyers impact.

Additionally, I learned the importance of balancing text and visuals to ensure readability while also capturing attention. This project helped me refine my design skills and reinforced the importance of attention to detail in creating professional, effective marketing materials.
