Bio

Hello! My name is Kenadi Adams, and I am currently enrolled in OHVA as a highschool student. I currently am unemployed, however I plan to get an internship as a Graphic Designer, and do paid commissions as a side job in my future. I have been in a CTE pathway for Interactive Media for a little longer than a year now and have learned so much about what it means to be a graphic designer. I am in a family of six people, being one of the two daughters. I have always had an interest with art, and during when the pandemic first started (2019/2020), I grew an interest for digital art, starting with just an old ipad and an app called 'Procreate'. I had not known exactly what digital art was, until I fully immersed myself in research about it. Eventually, I knew this was what I was passionate about, after years of pondering the age old question asked by many, "What do you want to be when you grow up". I would always think to myself when asked this "How in the world am I supposed to know?" It's a tough question for many, but I've learned that you find hobbies in life, that you never think could be anything more in the moment, until you do. After finding the Interactive Media CTE pathway, it sparked many opportunities for me, for which I am and will always be grateful for. I am constantly trying to learn more, and discover other talents in this field I may have, or would like to learn. I have and will continue to explore all of the possibilities that I can achieve with it.

My Favorite Shows

The Good Place
When Eleanor ShellstropKirsten Bell finds herself in the afterlife, she's both relieved and surprised that she's made it into the Good Place. But it doesn't take long for Eleanor to realize she's there by mistake. She hides in plain sight from the Good Place's architect, Michael Ted Danson,and his all-knowing assistant, Janet D'Arcy Carden. Her seemingly perfect neighbors, Tahani Jameela Jamil and Jason Manny Jacinto, and open-hearted soul mate, Chidi William Jackson Harper, help her realize that it's never too late to change. With the help of her new friends -- and a few enemies -- Eleanor becomes determined to shed her old way of life in hopes of discovering a new one in the afterlife.
Santa Clarita Diet
Drew Barrymore and Timothy Olyphant star in this Netflix-original series as married realtors, Sheila and Joel, who are living a quiet life, raising their teenage daughter in Santa Clarita, Calif. Their world unexpectedly changes when Sheila goes through a dramatic transformation that sends her down a road of death and destruction -- but leaves her looking and feeling better than ever. Barrymore and Olyphant also serve as executive producers, alongside showrunner and creator Victor Fresco ("Better Off Ted").
Jane The Virgin
The daughter of a teen mother, Jane Villanueva grew up determined not to repeat her mom's mistakes. At 23, her life is on track; Jane is studying to be a teacher and engaged to a handsome detective who supports her decision to remain a virgin until marriage. Then a routine clinic visit flips her life upside down. Inseminated by a specimen meant for a patient in the next room, now-pregnant Jane is in a situation made only more insane when she learns that the sperm donor is her boss, Rafael. As her meticulously planned life gets more like the telenovelas she loves, she faces a lot of complicated decisions about where to go from here.
