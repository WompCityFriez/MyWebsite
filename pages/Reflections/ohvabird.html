Ohio Virtual Academy Logo Design


About This Project

This project allowed me to gain proficiency in Adobe Illustrator while enhancing my creativity and resilience.

While taking my Interactive Media CTE class, I was tasked with designing a logo for Ohio Virtual Academy (OHVA) using Adobe Illustrator. This project taught me how to effectively combine design principles with brand identity. I focused on creating a logo that was visually appealing while clearly representing OHVAs values and mission.

I gained valuable hands-on experience with Adobe Illustrator, learning how to work with shapes, colors, and typography to create a cohesive and professional design. Through this project, I improved my skills in logo creation, focusing on simplicity, scalability, and how to design something that would resonate with the target audience.
