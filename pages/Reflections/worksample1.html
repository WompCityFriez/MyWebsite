Reflection on a Flyer for train ride


During this assignment I learned the importance of Typography. Fonts I chose are associated with the 1920's and help build a feeling of temporal placement. It gave me a better understanding of how typography can change, and the aspects of it. This assignment also helped me learn more about Adobe Illustrator. Along with all of the ways that you can alter and morph text, and how doing this can help accentuate the topic. This was one of my first assignments, and it was very tricky at some points, but it was eye opening. This assignment helped me gain more creativity and resilience for difficult tasks.
